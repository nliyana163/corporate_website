About

Trinity Children's Home

works and advocates to give children in institutions and unplanned newborn babies the opportunity to grow in the care of a family. We are located at number 5, Jalan Bukit Menteri Utara 7/4, Seksyen 7, 46050 Petaling Jaya, Selangor.

Trinity Children's Home welcomes every parents and donor to join our activities. You can get involved by doing charity to our home by giving donations.

Every donation given is very valuable for the children at Trinity Children's Home. Our staff will always strive to provide fullness to the Trinity Children's Home sacks.
